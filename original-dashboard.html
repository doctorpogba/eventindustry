Executive Summary

Key insights from the global events industry recovery and strategic outlook for leadership decision-making

📈

Massive Recovery

From pandemic collapse to $1.23T in 2024, approaching $1.34T in 2025. Exhibitions and corporate meetings have nearly regained pre-pandemic volumes.

🌍

Regional Powerhouse

Asia-Pacific leads with 10% CAGR, Europe at 9%, North America 8%. China and Southeast Asia drive expansion momentum globally.

🤖

Tech Revolution

AI adoption surged 60% in 2024, with 46% of organizers using AI tools. Event tech spending rose 30% year-over-year.

🌱

Sustainability Imperative

45% of organizers plan sustainable practices by 2025. Travel accounts for 50-90% of event emissions, driving green innovation.
